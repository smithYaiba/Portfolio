Crew

  • Fon (Artist)

Description

This turn-based game is all about a group of college students trying to skip class! But their professor is standing in their way. Team up with your classmates and battle your way out in pixel-art style turn-based combat. Created using Monogame, this Windows game is sure to be a blast!
